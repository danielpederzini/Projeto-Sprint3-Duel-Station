Yu-Gi-Oh's Story

Yu-Gi-Oh is a trade card game franchise, posteriorly addapted to anime series and videogames. The story takes place in a world where people use monster, magic and trap cards to duel against each other, wheter for fun or competition. However, there's an obscure and mystic past about the game, which few know about.





It all started in ancient egypt, when kings and pharaohs used to play a very similar game, but with real monsters and spells. Lots of people died and whole cities were destroyed by the duels. Unhappy with that, a brave pharaoh called Atem prisioned the darkness and magic of the game in 7 artifacts, freeing the humans from the despair. Until...





Most of these artifacts fell into human hands, including the protagonist's Yugi Muto ones. After solving the puzzle of his artifact (The Millenium Puzzle), Yugi summoned in his body the spirit of Atem, the pharaoh which saved the world. The mission now is to prevent the darkness from the other artifacts to come out and spread destruction again.
